About

This page presents a collection of photos taken during my trip to Berlin and Munich, Germany, where I studied the local language and attended matches of the UEFA Euro 2024. The images capture moments of cultural immersion, the excitement of the tournament, and the everyday life in those cities. It's a personal reflection on language learning, sports, and the rich cultural atmosphere of Berlin during this unique time.
